Tomato Pasta

Description

This is a simple tomato pasta.

Total preparation time is about an hour.

Ingredients

  • olive oil
  • garlic
  • salt
  • tomatoes
  • basil
  • pasta

Steps

  1. Gut and dice the tomatos
  2. Crush 5 or 6 cloves of garlic
  3. Chop fresh basil
  4. Heat olive oil in a pan
  5. Put a pan of well-salted water on to boil
  6. Fry garlic with a pinch of salt in oil until it starts to go golden
  7. Add tomatoes to pan, salt, stir, and bring to a simmer
  8. Add pasta to boiling water
  9. Add some pasta water to garlic, oil, tomatoes mix
  10. Once pasta is cooked, add pasta to tomato sauce
  11. Serve with grated parmigiana and chopped basil
